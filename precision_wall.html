Introducing the Precision Wall


The Precision Wall helps players develop their attacking attributes, like shooting accuracy, first-touch, and teamwork.



FINE-TUNE YOUR ATTACK




Working individually or in teams, players can access a range of drills designed to replicate the pressure of real match situations. The Precision Wall is great for practicing set pieces, and can even simulate the dive of a professional goalkeeper.

DATA PLATFORM (THIS WALL REALLY DOES TALK...)


The ESA data platform allows coaches to benchmark a player’s ability across different dimensions, and measure improvements over time.


Players can compete with themselves and each other across a range of training programmes, boosting motivation, and giving training an extra competitive element.


CHOOSE THE RIGHT SIZE FOR YOU


We offer two standard sizes:


• Full size (28-panel)


• 6-a-side (9-panel)


We are also open to custom builds when customers have specific requirements.


Panel appearance is fully customisable, with a range of colours and branding options available.


THE SMART PITCH


Customers can also link two Precision Wall's of any size together to create a Smart Pitch. This is perfect for small sided matches with the 9-panel Precision Wall, full-sided games with the 28-panel Wall or to link up with our Wireless Circuit for finishing training drills. The Precision Wall's advanced technology allows the user to have complete flexibility with set-up and a 28-panel Wall can be used as a 9-panel goal with ease.
