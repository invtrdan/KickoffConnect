GET CREATIVE


Our Wireless Circuit allows coaches to create training drills around a diverse range of tactical scenarios. Modular and completely wireless, coaches can make use of the preset drills, or create their own using any combination of modules. The possibilities are endless!


However you choose to set up the Wireless Circuit, realtime feedback is available via the data platform, giving coaches the insight they need to enhance training and boost performance. There are currently four Circuit panels, each with a unique set of training benefits:


• Master Module - A Rebound Panel with the Control Unit & Scoreboard

• Rebound Module - A Rebound Panel with the ESA Technology

• Pass-Through Module - Great for Passing to Teammates or Dribbling

• Gate Module - Motion Technology for Running with/without the Ball

MASTER MODULE


The Master Module is the control panel for any Wireless Circuit set-up.


It is essentially a Rebound Module, which allows the player to test their passing accuracy and runs off a battery or mains socket.


PASS-THROUGH MODULE


Fitted with LEDs and Beam Breaker technology, players must thread a pass through the arch, or knock the ball through to themselves.


Allows maximum fluidity during a drill.


GATE MODULE


The Gate Module simulates more realistic dribbling, and movement off-the-ball.


A Beam Breaker is fitted at waist height, which players must pass through with or without the ball, to satisfy the target.


REBOUND MODULE


Equipped with LED targets, timers and vibration sensors for real time feedback.


The Rebound Module lets players test their passing accuracy, as well as set up a realistic shooting opportunity.


GROUND BREAKING TECHNOLOGY


The Wireless Circuit was nominated for Best Technology for Injury Prevention or Rehabilitation at the Sports Technology Awards.


Created alongside professional coaches at one of the world's biggest clubs, the Circuit allows players to rehabilitate faster, removing the danger of injury recurrence.


PERFORMANCE DATA AT YOUR FINGERTIPS


An extra second here or there can be the difference between winning and losing.


The ESA data platform allows coaches to get hands on with performance data, spot weaknesses, and track improvements over time.


The Wireless Circuit can be controlled using virtually any smart device.
